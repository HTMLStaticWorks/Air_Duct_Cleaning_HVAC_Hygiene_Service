Who We Are

About AeroPure Hygiene Services

Learn about our mission, certifications, and our daily commitment to securing healthy indoor spaces.

Founded in 2006

Providing Clean Indoor Air Quality Solutions for Over Two Decades

AeroPure was established with a singular focus: to solve the commonly overlooked problem of contaminated indoor ventilation. Our founder, a mechanical engineer, discovered that many residential allergy complaints stemmed directly from uncleaned air ducts spreading pollen, dust, and pet dander.

Today, AeroPure has grown into a premier home hygiene service, executing over 15,000 project cleanings. We combine industrial-strength HEPA filtration with friendly local customer service to ensure you know exactly what is inside your vents.

Healthy Living Spaces

Our Vision for Safe, Allergen-Free Residential Environments

We understand that the safety of your family is paramount. Standard HVAC vents recirculate air multiple times daily, accumulating unseen irritants. If someone in your home experiences unexplained congestion, dry eyes, or frequent sneezing, dirty ducts are often the culprit.

Our residential hygiene programs focus on deep cleansing. We completely clear out structural trunk lines, sanitize coil assemblies, and install premium allergen filters, ensuring your family breathes air as nature intended.

Environmental Commitment

Eco-Efficiency & Our Carbon Footprint Pledge

At AeroPure, we recognize that healthy indoor air quality should not come at the expense of our planet. That is why we have pioneered energy-efficient cleaning protocols that help customers conserve power while securing sterile breathing air.

100% Botanical Disinfectants

We exclusively utilize plant-derived, non-toxic bio-sanitizers (EPA-certified) that naturally degrade without releasing hazardous VOCs.

Up to 21% HVAC Energy Savings

Removing strictures from coils and ducts decreases mechanical resistance. Your blower motor works less, reducing monthly utility energy consumption.

Particulate HEPA Containment

Negative pressure agitation vacuum systems trap 99.97% of fine debris. We prevent dust and micro-allergens from leaking into the outside environment.
