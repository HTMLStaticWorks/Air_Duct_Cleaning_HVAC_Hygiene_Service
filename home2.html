Invisible Airway Threats

Common Contaminants In Your Vents

If left uncleaned, air ducts become breeding grounds for structural pathogens and blockages that restrict HVAC system productivity.

Dust & Fiber Clogs

Fibrous lint and dander collect on filter tracks and fans, forcing motors to pull double electricity to heat/cool the room.

Mold & Spores

Cold air condensation creates damp surfaces in dark plenums, prompting microbial spore outbreaks that spread across office vents.

Pet Dander & Fur

Pet hair and airborne dander are pulled into return vents, recirculating allergens repeatedly through the internal air supply.

Vent Blockages

Debris, loose sheet metal insulation, or pest nests block duct channels, causing system imbalances and uneven room temperatures.
