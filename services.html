Step-By-Step Procedure

What Happens During Our Service Visit

We follow strict technical routines to clean your ducts without venting dust back into your living room.

PHASE 1

Containment & Setup

We seal off all ventilation registers with protective adhesive covers. The trunk line is then tapped and linked to our high-capacity HEPA negative air pressure vacuum to create draft.

PHASE 2

Agitation Scrubbing

Our technicians feed mechanical rotary brushes and air snakes down each register. The loose particles are pulled directly into the negative airflow channel, keeping your rooms dust-free.

PHASE 3

Sanitization & Testing

Once clean, we apply an EPA-registered botanic sanitising mist. We conclude the service by demonstrating optical before-and-after photographs and executing system pressure checks.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our professional ventilation and air quality treatments.

How often should my air ducts be cleaned?

According to NADCA standards, air ducts should be cleaned every 3 to 5 years. However, we recommend more frequent cleaning (every 2 years) for households with shedding pets, residents suffering from severe asthma or allergies, or immediately following home renovation projects.

How long does a standard cleaning appointment take?

For a standard single-family home with one heating and cooling system (10 to 15 register vents), a comprehensive cleaning takes approximately 3 to 4 hours. Larger properties, houses with multi-zone HVAC units, or systems requiring extensive AC coil sanitation may take longer.

Do I need to leave my home during the cleaning?

No, there is no need to vacate your home. We utilize advanced negative-pressure HEPA vacuums that capture 99.97% of particulates down to 0.3 microns. By sealing registers during agitation, we prevent loose dust from escaping into your living rooms, keeping the entire process dust-free.

Are your sanitizing sprays safe for children and pets?

Yes, absolutely. We exclusively use botanical, thyme-oil-based sanitizers that are EPA-registered and certified. They contain no chemical fumes, synthetic fragrances, or chlorine, making them completely safe for pets, infants, and allergy-sensitive individuals immediately after application.

How do I verify that the vents are actually clean?

We provide complete optical transparency. Before starting and after completing the cleaning process, our technicians use high-resolution endoscope duct cameras to capture direct visual proof of your structural duct cleanliness, which are shared with you in a final service report.
